Dutch food restaurants

Beyond the windmills and tulips the Netherlands has another cultural wonder to experience: its cuisine. So what is Dutch food exactly? Generally speaking, it’s simple and hearty with meat stews, soups, fish, seafood and frietjes (fries) featuring heavily on menus.
